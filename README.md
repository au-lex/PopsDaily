# 📰 PopsDaily

An ad-free, curated news aggregator built to bring back the joy of reading the news without the clutter—now featuring AI summaries and audio playback.

## 📖 The Story
I built this application for my dad. Growing up, I always watched him obsessed with reading the daily news. Lately, however, he's been complaining about how modern news platforms have become frustrating to navigate due to the sheer volume of intrusive ads and pop-ups. 

I created PopsDaily to give him back a clean, focused reading experience, pulling in his favorite local and international sources into one seamless interface. 

## ✨ Features
*   **Zero Ads:** A completely clean, distraction-free reading environment.
*   **Curated Aggregation:** Automatically fetches and formats articles from trusted sources, including:
    *   Daily Sun
    *   Vanguard
    *   BBC
*   **AI-Powered Summaries:** Automatically generates quick, concise summaries of longer articles for easy skimming.
*   **Voice Reader (Text-to-Speech):** Allows him to listen to the news hands-free whenever he feels like it.
*   **Cross-Platform Mobile UI:** Smooth, responsive, and native-feeling interface optimized for accessibility and easy reading.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend:** Flutter (Mobile App)
*   **Backend:** Node.js (API and web scraping/aggregation logic)
*   **AI & Audio:** [OpenAI API] for summaries and [Google Cloud TTS / Flutter native TTS] for the voice reader.
